How It Works

Turf Installed in 4 Simple Steps

From first call to finished yard — here's what the process looks like with Windsor Turf Pros.

Free On-Site Estimate

We visit your Windsor property, measure the area, and discuss your options. You receive a written quote before committing to anything.

Site Prep & Base Work

We remove existing grass or surface material, grade for drainage, and install a compacted crushed stone sub-base — the foundation of a lasting install.

Turf Cutting & Installation

The turf is cut precisely on-site, fitted to every edge, and secured with professional grade fastening. Seams are placed and finished so they're invisible.

Final Walkthrough

We inspect drainage, edges, and finish — and do a full walkthrough with you before we leave. The yard is ready to use the same day.

Common Questions About Artificial Turf in Windsor

Most residential turf projects in Ontario range from $8 to $18 per square foot installed, depending on turf grade and the complexity of site preparation. Final pricing depends on your specific yard size, surface type, and the product selected. We provide free written estimates with no obligation — contact us to get a number specific to your project.
Yes — modern artificial turf is engineered for Canadian climate conditions. The fibres, backing, and infill are all rated for freeze-thaw cycles. You can leave snow on the turf and it won't damage the surface. Most Windsor homeowners are pleasantly surprised by how well it looks coming out of winter.
Artificial turf can absorb heat on very sunny days, though shaded areas and pool-adjacent installations tend to stay cooler. For areas where heat is a concern — like a children's play space — we can discuss infill options that reduce surface temperature. A quick rinse with the hose cools the surface down in minutes.
The turf products we install are non-toxic, lead-free, and free of harmful chemicals. For pet areas, we recommend specific infill products that control odour and support good drainage. For play areas, we select turf with appropriate cushioning for falls.
In most cases, residential artificial turf installation in Windsor does not require a building permit. However, requirements can vary depending on the scope of the project — particularly if grading or drainage work is involved.
